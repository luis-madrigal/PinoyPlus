What is the PLHIV Response Center (PRC)?

PRC is a communication service that aims to provide an interactive platform for People Living with HIV (PLHIV) where they can voice out concerns as they seek treatment and other HIV-related services. PRC is also a case management tool for evidence-based advocacy and program development.

It delivers referrals and facilitates collaboration with treatment and service providers within the referral and service delivery network in response to the needs of the PLHIV community. These providers include social hygiene clinics, local government units, NGOs and CSOs, and psychosocial support groups.

Roles of PRC

1. Facilitate PLHlV's access to treatment, care and support setvices
2. Link the PLHIV to the appropriate service
3. Network with service providers to improve availability, accessibility and quality of
  services to PLHlVs
4. Document and evidence-building for advocacy and program development
